### Finance Completes Payment for the Invoiced Items

At this stage of the process, Finance has read-only access to all of the information populated throughout the process and has the ability to see everything as it has flowed.
Once the Finance Officer has reviewed all of the information, they can proceed to make their payments to the respective Vendor by utilizing the information in the process to guide their decision making.

Once this is done, the Finance Officer selects the option, _Complete Payment_, indicating that they payment was made to the Vendor. With this completed, the process is passed on to the Procurement Officer
once more to await their decision to guide the process.
